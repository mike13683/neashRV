About NICHE MOTORHOME HIRE

Welcome to NICHE MOTORHOME HIRE! We are an independent and leading campervan hire company that facilitates online comparison, selection and booking of motorhome rentals. Read the terms and conditions of respective supplier carefully before proceeding for the payment.

We believe in 100% Customer Satisfaction – We Live by It!

Our excellent customer service is a major parameter contributing to our smashing success. We continuously make attempts to fulfill all the needs and expectations of our targeted audience, so as to achieve highest customer satisfaction.

Our team helps you plan your itineraries in advance, so as to get the maximum of what holiday spot offers. We also allow you to book extras (additional accessories or say amenities in campervan) online.

Book with Confidence. OnRoadCampervan have well-trained staff on hand to assist you in each and every matter, right from the booking, road trip planning and campervan collection to returning of the vehicle.
